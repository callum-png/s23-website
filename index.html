About S23

Where Data
Meets
Culture.

S23 Operations Ltd is a London-based holding company building the infrastructure that powers modern brand growth. We own and operate a portfolio of companies at the intersection of marketing technology, creator media, and consumer software.

We don't run campaigns in isolation — we build the systems, proprietary tools, and expert teams that make growth scalable, measurable, and repeatable. Our subsidiaries work in concert, each reinforcing the other with first-party data and platform intelligence.

Think of us as an influencer marketing agency powered by data and strategy, hosted by our own software platform — with the reach of an agency and the precision of a technology company.
